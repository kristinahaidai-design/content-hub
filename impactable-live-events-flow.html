A1 — Entry Point
📣
External Traffic Source
Website banner, paid LinkedIn ads, email campaigns, or partner promotions drive clicks to a dedicated Impactable landing page — not LinkedIn's native event page.
A2 — Registration
📝
Custom Landing Page
Clean registration form: First name · Email · Company. On submit, pass these as URL parameters to the thank you page so the survey pre-fills — no re-entry required.
A3 — Thank You Page
✅
Survey Embedded on Thank You Page
Instead of "check your email," the page shows: "You're registered! Quick question while we send your confirmation..." and drops straight into the 4-question survey below.
B1 — Entry Point
💼
LinkedIn Organic — Justin's Audience
Justin posts about the live event. His followers see it in feed, click "Attend" directly on LinkedIn. LinkedIn handles registration natively — no redirect possible.
B2 — During the Live
🔴
In-Stream Survey Interception
Three interception moments during the LinkedIn Live stream:
📌
Pinned comment: Drop the survey link as a pinned comment the moment the stream starts. Keep it visible the entire session.
🎤
Verbal CTA (3×): Justin mentions it at the open, midpoint, and close — "Comment 'AUDIT' and I'll send you a free LinkedIn Ads audit."
🤖
ManyChat keyword DM: Anyone who comments "AUDIT" automatically receives a DM with the survey link. 0 manual effort, instant delivery.
B3 — Post-Event (within 1 hr)
📲
Follow-Up Post + Replay
Justin posts a recap immediately after. Catches replay viewers and anyone who missed the pinned comment. Includes the survey link with a clear audit CTA. Also repurposable as content.
🔀
Both tracks converge here — same survey, same routing logic
Track A gets the survey on the thank you page. Track B gets the survey link via pinned comment, ManyChat DM, or post-event post. From this point, the experience is identical.
