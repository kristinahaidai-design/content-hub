🎯 Tool Audience Definition — This Is Who We're Building For

Audience 1
Net-New LinkedIn Advertiser
Has never run LinkedIn ads. May be on Google, Meta, or other channels. Needs to know if LinkedIn makes financial sense for their business before committing a dollar. The question they're asking: "Should I even start?"
Audience 2
Existing Advertiser, New Market Entry
Already runs LinkedIn ads in one space. Now considering a new vertical, industry, or ICP they've never targeted. The question they're asking: "Will the math work in this new space before I commit test budget?"

⚠️ This tool is NOT for advertisers with an active LinkedIn strategy who are optimizing existing campaigns. That's a different product conversation entirely.

Recurring question from net-new advertisers across every marketing forum. The "right" answer isn't a fixed number — it's a function of deal value, close rate, and CPL range. The Audience 2 use case verbatim.
